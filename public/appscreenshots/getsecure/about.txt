About this app
Protect your privacy without babysitting another app.

GetSecure is a lightweight Android permission manager that scans your installed apps on demand, highlights risky permissions, and shows you exactly how to lock things down, no constant background monitoring, no battery drain.

Open the app, get a full permission scan, and see where your data is exposed.

Why users love GetSecure

On-demand permission scans
GetSecure only scans when you open it. No 24/7 background services, no silent tracking—just clear, instant results.

Clear privacy dashboard
View user apps and system apps separately. Track high, medium, and low-risk permissions at a glance, and mark trusted apps as Safe to keep your signal clean.

Weekly privacy report
Get a Monday summary showing new installs, permission changes, and overall privacy health score trends.

Permission drill-downs
Tap any permission group; camera, microphone, location, storage, notifications and see every app with access. Jump straight to Android settings to revoke or adjust permissions.

Trusted app tracking
Flag banking, finance, or utility apps as trusted so GetSecure highlights only the surprises that matter.

• One-time upgrade available to remove ads completely
